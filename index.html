Rules

When the timer starts, each player searches the assortment of letters for words of four letters or more. When you find a word, write it down.

Words are formed from adjoining letters. Letters must join in the proper sequence to spell a word. They may join horizontally, vertically or diagonally to the left, right or up-and-down. No letter cube, however, may be used more than once within a single word.

Squares marked with a "?" are wildcards. You may choose any letter to fill in the blank. The same letter must be used in all words formed using the "?".

When time is up, players take turns reading off the words in their lists. If two or more players wrote the same word, all players remove it from their lists.

Once all players have read their lists, the remaining words are scored according to the table below. The player with the highest score wins the round.

Proper nouns, abbreviations, contractions, hyphenated words, and foreign words that are not in an English dictionary are not allowed.
